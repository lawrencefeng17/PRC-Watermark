I pushed open the heavy wooden door and stepped inside, the chill of the night air melting away as I gazed up at the towering chandeliers that hung like candelabras from the hall above. I had been summoned to Ravenswood Manor by the police department, and as I stepped across the creaking floorboards into the foyer, I felt a shiver run down my spine. The silence was oppressive, heavy with the scent of decay and rot. I made my way through the dark staterooms and into the grand ballroom, where the rooms seemed to transition seamlessly into one another. The room itself was a maze of dimly lit corridors and dusty passageways, with no clear exit point.
